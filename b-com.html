B.COM Program offered by GN Group of Institutes, is affiliated to Chaudhary Charan Singh University Meerut. Bachelor of Commerce is three years graduation program, and we provide the best placement after B.Com. in Delhi NCR

A Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is a graduation, we provides the best theoretical as well as practical knowledge to the students related to the recent market, trade and commerce for business in Delhi/NCR.

B.Com is designed to help students to gain a better understanding of general business management functions. B.Com degree can have a general focus or a specific focus in fields such as Trade, E-Commerce, marketing, and relationship management.

B.Com program typically includes core classes in accounting, management, finance, marketing, and business law. Management training is at the heart of any B.Com. curriculum, with a focus on leadership, planning, business strategy, organizational behavior, Trade, E-Commerce and the more human sides of running a large or small business.

Eligibility

Candidate must have passed class 12th or equivalent with min 33% Commerce is mandatory or 38% Art & Science Candidate from any recognized board.
