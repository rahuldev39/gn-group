About Us

About Us

GN Group of Institutes started in 2008 with its lush campus in Knowledge park-II, Greater Noida.

GN Group of Institutes seeks to transform higher education in India by creating a world class Innovating teaching, learning, and research environment.

The Institute is borne out of the four main pillars of an Educational FISA namely…. Faculty, Infrastructure, Students and Ambience.GN Group of Institute is one of the best Colleges in Delhi NCR.

Focusing on the four aspects an unquenchable thirst for innovation and disruption; a rich legacy of technology and entrepreneurship; and a commitment to nurturing leaders who make a differences to the world has been the credo of GN GROUP in the nearly 13 years of its existence.

GN GROUP of Institutes attracts students from all over India.

The institute has an array of well qualified and spirited faculty members. Who have a zest for quality education and growth of the students. There have been many Research papers and publications by our esteemed faculty members in various Journals of National and International repute.

The campus boasts of a serene enivironment, well tuned for the students 39; every need. It has state of the art laboratories and equipments for all the courses we offer. Well suited for imparting quality of higher education.

The institute has a good quality recreational and sports program for the Students. Along with many co- curricular activities like fine arts, music, dance and other performing arts.

Chairman Message

Dear Students & Parents, We at GN Group of Institutes, welcomes you all in our dedicated journey to provide an optimum blend of academics, professionalism, personality grooming and intimate industry interface as evident in our logo “YOUR PASSION OUR MISSION”. We believe in providing life experience based teaching and learning techniques. We try our best to recruit the best faculty, induct them properly and help them to imbibe Excel Culture and value and serve the students and industry with great dedication. The highly pragmatic and progressive environment developed by the talented faculty makes substantial contribution to the academia through quality teaching, publications, seminars, conferences etc. The corporate interactions including corporate supported projects undertaken by our students under faculty supervision is considered ‘unique’ in the sense, that it provides a close hands-on-experience to our students as part of our curriculum. I am sure any student entering the portals of this college would be a proud alumnus, when they graduate from here .I wish you all the best for your future.

Vision

An academic community dedicated to the holistic education of future leaders who will demonstrate innovation in their professional competencies, integration in their personal lives and inclusion in their social contribution.

Mission

Our mission Stems from our vision with A three pronged approach to cater to the needs of students and the Industry.

  • Ensuring competence, and provoking excellence and brilliance
  • Stimulating critical and creative thinking
  • Promoting multi-disciplinary learning, research and publication
  • Linking studies with contemporary industry developments and applications
  • Making core competencies socially and environmentally beneficial
  • Fostering confidence, motivation and vision
  • Upholding honesty, transparency and accountability
  • Facilitating emotional, interpersonal, social and spiritual intelligence
  • Developing organizational and leadership skills
  • Encouraging creative and collaborative engagement in curricular and co/extra-curricular activities
  • Respecting the dignity of others and practicing compassion and concern
  • Appreciating cultural pluralism and diversity
  • Striving for social justice, harmony and solidarity
  • Caring and providing for the marginalized and disadvantaged
  • Strategizing to protect the natural environment
