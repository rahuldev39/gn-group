GN Group Of Institutes offers three year Diploma Program in the following stream:

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering-Automobile

Courses are approved by All India Council For Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and affiliated to Board of Technical Education, Lucknow(UP). We have a technical arena for training & placement. Academic and professional activities with technology like conferences, seminars and workshops etc. are conducted on regular basis. GN Group of Institutes is one of the best polytechnic colleges in Delhi NCR. Technical journals and magazines provided through the library enhance the knowledge about the latest researches and advancements in academic and industry front. In addition to the experimental work in laboratories and workshops, the students are exposed to Industrial practices through guided tours to different Industries.

Diploma In Computer Science & Engineering

IT industries are booming in our country and with this, the requirement of software engineers is at an all-time high. Diploma in Computer Engineering course thus has got great career prospects and job opportunities not just within the country, but worldwide.

Information technology-related start-ups are given a major boost by the government and investors. The field of Computer Science has thus seen a great jump in terms of skill development potential such as in the line of writing codes, application programming, mobile operating systems, related soft skills, etc. GN Group of Institutes also has a world-class learning resource facility that enables students to take up Additional Certification on topics such as computer operating systems (OS), programming languages such as C programming, Java, Python, etc.

Diploma In Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineers are needed in all manufacturing units; all major companies of the public and private sector such as Electricity Boards, Large Scale Industries, Manufacturing Plants, Power Corporations, Hydro-Electricity sector and Railways are continuously in need of Electrical Engineers for their projects.

The Various Projects (Outdoor/Indoor) are fabricated and developed as per the syllabus guidelines in the laboratories. In the project activity, the students are trained to fabricate working models apart from conventional ones like Voltage Stabilizers, Automatic Controllers, Power Inverters, and Digital Clock Etc. This practice creates an innovation and development attitude among the young students.

Diploma In Mechanical Engineering

Our Mechanical department is committed to train the students for achieving the goal to accomplish the production processes in a smooth, most judicious and most economical way.

The course aims at providing the fecund brains with fundamental knowledge in Mechanical Engineering. Instructions are imparted in the areas of refrigeration and air conditioning, Design of mechanical components and machines, techniques of management, computer science, CAD/CAM. Design and fabrication projects are also undertaken as a part of the curriculum.

Eligibility

Matriculation (10th) or equivalent from a recognized Board. For lateral entry candidate should be 12th (PCM) or ITI.

DEPARTMENT HIGHLIGHTS

Infrastructure

The campus is decked with a serene environment, well-tuned for the students and their needs. It has state-of-the-art laboratories and equipment for all the courses we offer.

Career Opportunities

Diploma (Computer Science & Engineering)

After successful completion of this Course, students will be able to present professional and ethical responsibilities and will have the technical skill sets to make effective use of latest engineering tools, multiple software, and a technique for engineering practices. The course will provide education with expansiveness that allows for the understanding of the impact of engineering practices on all aspects of society and an ability to work in multidisciplinary environments to manage technical projects and to succeed in different competitive exams. After Completion of this Programme students can get job as:

  • Jr. Software Engineer
  • Hardware Engineer
  • Networking Engineer
  • Software Tester
  • Web Designer
  • App Developer
  • Jr. Project Engineer

Diploma (Electrical Engineering)

After Completion of Diploma in Electrical Engineering students will get Job Opportunities in either Private and Govt. Sector both.

Some of the private companies offering jobs for diploma holders in Electrical Engineering are IBM, HCL, Philips, I-Gate, Global Solutions, Wipro, Pinnacle, Siemens, Samsung, Info-Com, Motorola, Kshema Technologies, Kirloskar, TISCO, L&T, ABB etc.

EEE diploma holders can also apply for jobs in the public sector also. Some of the public and Govt. sectors where they can look for a job are NTPC, DVC, TELCO, Government Electricity board, BHEL, Air Traffic Control Center (A.T.C.), HAL, Railways, Indian Air Force, HMT, Indian Navy, Civil Aviation Department, Steel Authority of Indian Limited (SAIL).

Diploma (Mechanical Engineering - Automobile)

Diploma in Automobile Engineering is a specialised branch of Mechanical Engineering, which covers the areas of automobile engines, transmission, suspension, braking systems, electrical and electronics, comfort and safety, automotive workshop technology and management. This course deals with diploma in ME- Automobile Engineering to impart quality education and training on how to handle inside and out of auto-motives and working of their mechanical parts. This program aims to create diploma holders in automobile engineering with knowledge of vehicle engineering, incorporating elements of mechanical, electrical, electronics, software and safety engineering applied to the design, manufacture and operation of motorcycle, automobile, buses and trunks and their respective engineering subsystem.

After successful Completion of this course, multiple opportunities are available in automobile industries Companies such as Honda, Toyota, Maruti Suzuki, Bajaj Motors, TVS, Hyundai, Mahindra and Mahindra etc. offer employment opportunities for Diploma in ME-Automobile Students.
