APTITUDE TRAINING

In Aptitude Training, we provide trainings to the student by which they improve their Quantitative Ability, Logical Ability, Verbal Ability, and basic Computer’s Knowledge. We conduct Mock Test based on Aptitude Skills, by which we can assess the student’s Quantitative and logical Ability to draw conclusion from a given set of information. For English (Verbal Ability), we are offering English Language Labs, where, students will good grasp on the grammar. For Computers, we provide Computer Labs, where, we master students in computers, where, they learn basic computer’s knowledge, such as, MS WORD, MS OFFICE, MS EXCEL, and POWERPOINT PRESENTION, etc.

PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

It is a training process in which, trainer helps to the students in enhancing their Personality. In PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT TRAINING, we not only help to improve the Communication Skills of the students, but also, we influence many other areas of the student’s personality like Time Management Skills, Team Management Skills, Decision Making Skills, and Leadership Skills, etc. For Personality Development, we prepare students for the Interviews and group discussion, and also conduct Mock Test, Mock Interviews, Group discussion, Events and Activities related to personality development {such as, ICE BREAKING GAMES and Team building games, etc.}, English Language Labs, Profiling of Students, Workshop on Stress Management, Workshop on Resume Making, Workshops on Motivation, and Workshops on Body Language, etc.
