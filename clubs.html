SPORTS CLUB

The aim behind the formation of sports club is to promote and develop interest in a particular sport or physical activity. Because sports can provide the basic for a lifelong participation in regular exercise, and the development of mental and moral qualities, as well as team spirit, self-discipline, and cooperation. Club organizes the sports event, both Intra as well as Inter college competitions.

SOCIAL CLUB

The Aim behind the formation of social club is to serve the society, by helping to the needy people in different ways, like providing them food, clothes, books and stationary, etc., and it exists for voluntary or charitable activities. Club organizes the events for the welfare of the society in which members of the club educate and support the people of the weaker section of the society in many ways.

CULTURAL CLUB

The aim behind the formation of cultural club is to meet regularly to explore different creative and cultural activities. Club organizes the events within the college, in which students show their interest and skills to others, and the competition is on Intra as well as Inter level.

TECHNICAL CLUB

The aim behind the formation of Technical Club is to organize the activities like which are based or related to new and old technology. Club members organizes activities like Model Making, Robo-games, Coding competitions, Poster Presentation, CAD Competition, Technical Quiz, Project Exhibition is on Intra as well as Inter level.

ACADEMIC CLUB

The aim behind the formation of Academic Club is to organize the activities for students, which are based on their particular topic of Interest. This improves the group development management and Public speaking skills. Club organizes the events related to the Academics like Quiz Competitions, Puzzle Competitions, Artist Hub, etc.
