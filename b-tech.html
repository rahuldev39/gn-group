Course Overview

Duration Department Intake Affiliation Approvals
4 years (8 semesters) Computer Science and Engineering 240 Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University AICTE

B.Tech offered by GN Group Of Institutes is 4 year full time program approved by All India Council For Technical Education (AICTE),New Delhi and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow, UP.

GN Group Of Institutes is one of the best Engineering Colleges in Delhi NCR.

To build a strong learning and research environment in the field of computer science and engineering that promotes innovation towards the betterment of the society. To produce computer science graduates who are trained in design and implementation of computational systems through competitive curriculum and research in collaboration with industry and research organizations.

Highlights

  • Computer science Engineering syllabus keeps pace with the evolving industry needs and technologies like Artificial Intelligence, machine learning, Data science, Cloud Computing, Robotics and cyber security enhancing the prospects for higher studies and multi-disciplinary research.
  • Get 15 + unique certification in life skills from Industry partner during B.Tech progression towards professional competency development activity.
  • Department of CSE offers state of the art infrastructure, center of excellence, qualified faculty members and excellent Industry academic interactions to provide vibrant environment for the students to evolve themselves into sustainable engineers of tomorrow.
  • Department of Computer Science Engineering is one of the best departments in GN Group of Institutes with excellent edge Internship opportunities.

Eligibility

Candidates must have passed 10+2 examination or its equivalent from any recognized Board/ University with at least 45% marks for General Category 40% marks for SC/ST Category in Mathematics, Physics and one optional subject of Chemistry /Biology/Computer Science.

Through UPESS Counseling: for the Academic Year 2021, the Score sheet of JEE (Main) will be used for admission to B.tech Course.

For B.Tech (Lateral)-2nd Year- Candidate must have passed diploma with at least 45% marks for General Category 40% marks for SC/ST Category.
