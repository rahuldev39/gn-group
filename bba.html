BBA program offered by GN Group of Institutes, is affiliated to Chaudhary Charan Singh University Meerut. Bachelor of Business Administration is three- year undergraduate program.

BBA Graduates can either launch their professional career in top corporate, become entrepreneurs or progress to pursue a PG program. The program offers GN Group students a core of mandatory courses in General Business Law, Economics, Finance, Marketing and HR Management.

Todayâ€™s corporate world values Critical Thinking, Logical Reasoning, Problem Definition and Solution Building in a resource. All these qualities are some of the basic qualities that are expected in a Manager.

Our BBA program has a built-in Life Skills module that not only takes care of these basic skills but also enhances students Teamwork and Leadership Skills.

Eligibility

Candidate must have passed class 12th or equivalent with min 45% (for General category) and 40% (for SC/ST) from any recognized board.
